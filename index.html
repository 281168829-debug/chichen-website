About Tianjin Chichen Trading

Based in the industrial hub of Tianjin, Tianjin Chichen Trading Co., Ltd. is a premier global supplier of high-performance cordless power tools. We serve distributors and wholesalers across the European market and the "Belt and Road" regions with products that meet rigorous quality standards.

Our products are CE, RoHS, and FC certified, manufactured in ISO 9001-compliant facilities. We focus on the ONE 21V battery platform — a single battery that powers 60+ tools across 15 categories, reducing inventory complexity for our partners.

Every CHICHEN tool undergoes a full-load stress test before packaging. We stand behind our products with responsive after-sales support and a commitment to continuous improvement.
